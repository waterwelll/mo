Ruth Jhinnabhoy

Ruth Jhinnabhoy always knew she was destined for a career in the entertainment industry. With a deep passion for storytelling and sound, she pursued Media Studies at Boston Media House in 2017, majoring in Radio. Born and raised in Lenasia South, Ruth spent much of her time immersed in books, music, and poetry—developing a creative voice that would later shape her career. Her love for music naturally led her into the world of music compiling, where she curates sounds that resonate with diverse audiences. At the same time, her fascination with radio helped her uncover a talent for voice-over artistry, allowing her to bring stories to life through her captivating vocal presence. With a unique blend of skills in media and entertainment, Ruth continues to carve out a space for herself, driven by creativity, passion, and an undeniable love for sound.
